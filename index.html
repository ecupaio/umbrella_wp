Umbrella Consulting, LLC is a boutique fundraising and communications consulting firm devoted to helping nonprofits and political campaigns raise the funds they need to take their mission to the next level.

Our services can be used individually or as part of a comprehensive package. We’re committed to working one-on-one with potential clients to develop a tailored, affordable plan that will result in measurable, long-term financial growth.

What we do
Capital Campaigns

Running a capital campaign can be tricky and risky in that you want to make sure you’re not raising money for one particular project only to find you’ll have a shortfall on your general budget.  We can help you properly budget your project, strategize the rollout, and identify those donors most likely to make a special gift.

Social Media

While Millennials are generally not yet capable of giving the same substantial gifts older generations are, they’re are proving to be incredibly philanthropic and willing to give their time and energy to a cause they care about.  If you want their inclusion in your cause, you have to be where they are: social media.  We can guide you through the best practices for social media platforms, including Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram, to appeal to younger generations.

Direct Mail

Anyone who tells you direct mail is a dying practice is badly misinformed.  It’s true that online fundraising is growing every year, but it’s still miles behind the cornerstone that is direct mail for most organizations.  We can write you direct mail that packs a punch and will bring in more money.

Major Donor Cultivation

Our specialty at Umbrella Consulting is helping our clients establish personal relationships with their major donors.  Whether through one-on-one conversations over coffee to small-scale events, building relationships with major donors has proven paramount to maintaining and upgrading their support year-after-year. We will guide you every step of the way through understanding the best practices for stewarding, soliciting and acknowledging these critical supporters.

Prospect Research

Finding new donors can be an exhausting exercise that results in little return on investment.  Aside from that, how many of your current small-dollar donors have the capacity to be giving substantially more and how do you identify who they are?  Using wealth screening technology, we can explore an individual’s past philanthropic and political giving, wealth indicators, and personal backgrounds.  Supplied with this information, you will know exactly who to ask and what to ask for.  We can either screen prospects en masse or as part of a larger major donor cultivation package.

Online Communications

While still far behind direct mail, online fundraising is growing every year and it’s the best instrument for securing monthly donors.  We can write you hard-hitting online communications and make sure your website is properly prepared to take contributions, including recurring contributions from donors who want to give monthly.

Planned Giving

Planned giving, also called legacy giving or estate planning, is an oft-ignored pillar of any fundraising operation. More and more donors are becoming interested in how they can leave a legacy and your nonprofit must be prepared to make a case for stewarding that legacy. We can help your nonprofit either build a planned giving program from scratch or invigorate one you already have in place through smart marketing, precise donor research, and proper relationship-building.

Board Development

Aside from guiding the mission of a nonprofit, boards can be powerful engines of fundraising. Whether through personal contributions, personal connections, or helping cultivate and thank donors, every board member has a role to play and we can help them to realize their full potential, as well as to understand fundraising strategies.  We can work with board members one-on-one or provide classroom-like lectures and workshops.

Bio

Jonathan Kellam is a leading fundraising consultant with over a decade of experience partnering with nonprofits and political campaigns.  Getting his start “dialing for dollars” as a volunteer, Jonathan quickly developed into a major fundraising force in Florida politics where he worked on local, state and congressional Democratic campaigns.

After moving to Washington, D.C., Jonathan worked for a large nonprofit organization where he focused on major donor development to comprise an annual budget of $15 million while also securing an additional $15 million in planned gifts.  Since founding Umbrella Consulting, Jonathan has helped numerous nonprofits and campaigns understand what truly drives donors – proper stewardship that results in real relationships and ensuring every gift is acknowledged appropriately.

Jonathan holds a BA in Political Science from the University of Central Florida and an MPS in Political Management from The George Washington University.  He is also a decorated veteran of the Army National Guard.  When he’s not raising money, Jonathan frequently speaks at conferences and on college campuses around the country.
